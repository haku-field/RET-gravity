# Misuse Patterns

The following distort RET-gravity into non-compliant forms.

## Destiny laundering
Structural non-return is rewritten as inevitability or promised convergence.

Result:
gravity becomes fate narrative.

## Attraction overwrite
Pull contours are reframed as emotional or ontological binding.

Result:
parallel condition collapses into mythology.

## Lock-in projection
Unavailable return paths are used to justify irreversible downstream commitments.

Result:
non-authoritative status is lost.

## Residue overwrite
Cancellation traces are mistaken for preserved truth.

Result:
gravity loses observational neutrality.
