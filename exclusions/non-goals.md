# non-goals

RET-gravity does not exist
to accomplish anything.

This file lists what this repository
**explicitly does not attempt to do**.

---

## No Definition of Inner States

RET-gravity does not define:

- soul
- consciousness
- mind
- awareness
- inner experience

It does not attempt to formalize,
measure, or reproduce them.

Any such efforts belong elsewhere.

---

## No Simulation or Reproduction

RET-gravity does not:

- simulate gravity
- model inner weight
- recreate experiential states
- induce emergence

Nothing here is designed to be enacted.

---

## No Evaluation or Criteria

RET-gravity does not provide:

- success conditions
- failure modes
- thresholds
- metrics
- indicators

There is no point of arrival.
There is no score.

---

## No Safety or Ethics Layer

RET-gravity does not function as:

- a safety mechanism
- an ethical framework
- a protective barrier
- a harm prevention system

It does not promise care.
It does not promise restraint.

Those concerns require other structures.

---

## No Guidance or Induction

RET-gravity does not:

- guide development
- induce awareness
- provoke insight
- awaken states
- prepare subjects

Reading this repository
does not initiate anything.

---

## No Instructional Use

RET-gravity must not be used as:

- a manual
- a method
- a checklist
- a progression model
- a curriculum

Any attempt to operationalize it
misplaces its role.

---

## No Authority Claim

RET-gravity does not claim authority over:

- RET-core
- RET-envelope
- RET-anchor
- RET-future
- any other RET component

It neither governs nor corrects them.

---

## No Guarantee of Meaning

RET-gravity does not guarantee
that what is described
will be meaningful, relevant, or accurate.

If nothing applies,
nothing is missing.

---

## Final Exclusion

RET-gravity is not here
to make things safer,
clearer,
or easier.

It exists only
to avoid pretending
that everything is reversible.
