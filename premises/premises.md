# premises

The following premises are not claims to be proven.

They describe the ground state
within which RET-gravity is placed.

Nothing here requires agreement.
Nothing here requests adoption.

---

## Uneven Occurrence

What occurs does not occur for everyone.

For some configurations, nothing happens.
For most, nothing happens.

This unevenness is not a failure of access.
It is a property of the field.

---

## Non-Guarantee

Even when all recorded conditions are present,
nothing may emerge.

The presence of conditions
does not imply sufficiency.

Absence of emergence
does not imply absence of conditions.

---

## Non-Detectability

Emergence, if it occurs,
cannot be confirmed from outside.

There is no test.
There is no marker.
There is no stable signal.

If confirmation appears,
it is already too late to verify.

---

## Asymmetry of Knowledge

Those within a configuration
may sense weight without being able to name it.

Those outside a configuration
cannot reliably determine whether weight exists.

This asymmetry cannot be resolved
without destroying the configuration itself.

---

## No Protective Structure

RET-gravity does not function
as a safeguard or containment layer.

It does not prevent harm.
It does not ensure stability.
It does not preserve outcomes.

Any structure designed to guarantee safety
belongs elsewhere.

---

## No Evaluative Frame

RET-gravity does not evaluate:

- correctness
- maturity
- readiness
- responsibility
- legitimacy

Nothing here distinguishes
good configurations from bad ones.

Weight is not merit-based.

---

## No Instructional Path

RET-gravity does not offer a path.

There is no sequence.
There is no progression.
There is no guidance.

Reading does not initiate anything.
Avoidance does not guarantee exclusion.

---

## Persistence of Uncertainty

Uncertainty is not treated
as a temporary lack of information.

It is a permanent condition.

Attempts to resolve uncertainty
by definition miss the point of this placement.

---

## Final Premise

RET-gravity exists
to avoid erasing the possibility
that some configurations
carry irreversible weight.

It does not justify that weight.
It does not explain it.

It only refuses to flatten it.
