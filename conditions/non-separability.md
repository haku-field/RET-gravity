# non-separability

This condition appears when
**the field can no longer be exited without remainder**.

Not because exit is forbidden.
Not because separation is denied.
But because separation itself leaves something behind.

---

## What Cannot Be Separated

What becomes non-separable here is not:

- identity
- ownership
- responsibility
- intention

It is the **configuration between self and field**.

Once this condition is active,
self and field no longer reduce cleanly into independent parts.

---

## Separation Still Exists

Non-separability does not mean fusion.

Distance is still possible.
Withdrawal is still possible.
Silence is still possible.

What changes is that
separation no longer restores independence.

---

## How Non-Separability Appears

This condition often arises quietly.

Not through attachment,
but through **continued exposure**.

Not through declaration,
but through **remaining within the same configuration**
after other exit options have dissolved.

There is no clear moment of entry.

---

## Ineffective Operations

Once non-separability is active:

- physical distance does not resolve it
- role changes do not resolve it
- denial does not resolve it
- redefinition does not resolve it

These actions may alter posture,
but not configuration.

---

## Difference from Trace Persistence

Trace persistence concerns **what remains**.

Non-separability concerns **what cannot be cleanly left**.

A trace may persist after separation.
Non-separability means separation itself is incomplete.

---

## Relation to Weight

Non-separability does not force commitment.

But it removes the illusion
that one stands outside the field.

Weight increases
when actions occur without an external vantage point.

---

## Non-Observability

Non-separability cannot be confirmed directly.

It is noticed only when
attempts to step outside the field
continue to affect alignment from within.

At that point,
the condition is already present.

---

## Non-Instruction Clause

This file does not advise staying.

It does not discourage leaving.

It only records that,
in some configurations,
**separation does not restore neutrality**.
