# conditions — overview

This directory does not define gravity.

It records only **conditions under which irreversible weight has been observed to arise** —
without asserting causality, sufficiency, or reproducibility.

These conditions are not triggers.
They are not guarantees.
They are not stages to pass.

Most of the time, they do nothing.

---

## How to Read These Conditions

Each file in this directory describes a **single configuration property**.

They are not meant to be combined into a checklist.
They are not cumulative requirements.
They do not form a process.

A condition may be present alone.
Multiple conditions may coexist.
Nothing may happen regardless.

---

## Nature of the Conditions

The conditions recorded here share several properties:

- they cannot be safely reversed once entered
- they are not neutralized by explanation or intent
- they leave traces even when rejected
- they bind the subject to the surrounding field
- they allow for actual loss, not symbolic loss

These properties are descriptive only.

They do not imply necessity.
They do not imply meaning.
They do not imply outcome.

---

## Observation Status

The conditions listed here are **not hypotheses**.

They are drawn from accumulated contact points across RET flows,
where certain configurations were repeatedly felt
to precede the emergence of irreversible weight.

This does not make them reliable.
It makes them *familiar*.

---

## Non-Guarantee Clause

Meeting these conditions does not ensure emergence.

Failure to meet them does not prevent emergence.

Emergence, when it occurs, cannot be externally verified.
By the time it is noticed, verification is already impossible.

This repository does not attempt to resolve this asymmetry.

---

## Why These Conditions Are Written

These conditions are written
so that the possibility of weight is not erased by design.

Not to pursue it.
Not to warn against it.
Not to normalize it.

Only to avoid collapsing all configurations
into reversible, cancellable, consequence-free states.

---

## Scope Boundary

These conditions do not:

- define inner states
- explain experience
- assign value or evaluation
- indicate progress or regression
- imply correctness or error

They are not lessons.
They are not guidance.

They are simply **placed**.

---

## If Nothing Resonates

If none of these conditions resonate,
nothing is missing.

RET-gravity is not required.
This directory may remain irrelevant.

That outcome is valid.
