# placement

RET-gravity is placed **after** something has already happened.

Not as a consequence.
Not as a reaction.
Not as an explanation.

Only as a layer that can exist
once return, cancellation, and separation are no longer guaranteed.

---

## Structural Position

RET-gravity is not upstream of any RET layer.

It does not ground:
- legacy fragments
- implementation-oriented specs
- derived modules or extensions

It does not constrain them,
and it must not be referenced as an authority.

Its position is strictly **suffix-like**.

If RET has layers that can be entered,
RET-gravity is a layer that may only be noticed
*after* entry elsewhere has already occurred.

---

## Placement Logic

RET-gravity is placed where:

- decisions cannot be undone
- words cannot be neutralized
- traces persist regardless of intent
- the surrounding field cannot be exited
- relations may actually be lost

This placement does not imply causality.

The presence of these conditions
does not ensure anything will emerge.

Most of the time, it does not.

---

## Non-Intervention Clause

RET-gravity does not intervene.

It does not:
- slow progression
- halt escalation
- protect participants
- stabilize relations
- prevent damage

Any structure designed to *manage* such outcomes
belongs elsewhere.

RET-gravity remains passive by design.

---

## Visibility

The state RET-gravity points toward
cannot be externally confirmed.

There is no reliable marker.
There is no observable threshold.
There is no reversible test.

If visibility appears,
it is already too late to verify.

---

## Reason for Placement

RET-gravity exists
so that **the possibility of weight is not erased**.

Not to ensure it.
Not to induce it.
Not to warn about it.

Only to avoid flattening everything
into reversible, cancellable, safe states.

---

## Discardability

RET-gravity may be ignored.

It may remain unread.
It may be removed.
It may never apply.

Its presence is not required
for RET to function.

Its absence does not break anything.

---

## Final Note

RET-gravity is not a destination.

It is not something to reach.

It is only the acknowledgement
that some configurations,
once entered,
cannot be exited without loss.
